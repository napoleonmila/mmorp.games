Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game

MMORP.GAMES is a website dedicated to keeping a record of the history and new developments of MMORPGs. you may already know, an MMORPG is a video game that combines elements of role-playing and massively multiplayer online games.

It's a genre that has captured the hearts and minds of millions of gamers around the world. Our website is designed to serve as a resource for all things MMORPG-related, providing information on the latest games, as well as preserving the history of this exciting and ever-evolving genre. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer to the world of MMORPGs, MMORP.GAMES is the perfect destination for all your gaming needs.

Top 10 MMORPGs

World of Warcraft

    World of Warcraft, often abbreviated as WoW, is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) set in the Warcraft universe. It was released in 2004 by Blizzard Entertainment and remains one of the most popular MMORPGs to date, with over 100 million accounts created. WoW features a vast, open world that players can explore and complete quests in, as well as a deep crafting system, challenging raid content, and a player-versus-player (PvP) system.

Final Fantasy XIV

    Final Fantasy XIV is a MMORPG developed and published by Square Enix. It is set in the Final Fantasy universe and was released in 2010. FFXIV boasts a highly engaging story, a vast and detailed world to explore, a crafting system, and an engaging community. It also features some of the most challenging PvE content in any MMORPG, including raids and dungeons.

Guild Wars 2

    Guild Wars 2 is a MMORPG developed by ArenaNet and published by NCsoft. It was released in 2012 and has since become one of the most popular MMORPGs on the market. GW2 is unique in that it features a dynamic world that changes based on player actions, as well as an innovative PvP system that pits players against each other in large-scale battles.

Elder Scrolls Online

    Elder Scrolls Online is a MMORPG developed by ZeniMax Online Studios and published by Bethesda Softworks. It was released in 2014 and is set in the Elder Scrolls universe. ESO features a vast and detailed world to explore, a deep crafting system, challenging PvE content, and an engaging PvP system.

Star Wars: The Old Republic

    Star Wars: The Old Republic is a MMORPG developed by BioWare and published by Electronic Arts. It was released in 2011 and is set in the Star Wars universe. SWTOR features a highly engaging story, a vast and detailed world to explore, a crafting system, and challenging PvE content. It also features a unique morality system that allows players to make choices that affect the story and their character's alignment.

Black Desert Online

    Black Desert Online is a MMORPG developed by Pearl Abyss and published by Kakao Games. It was released in 2016 and features a vast and detailed world to explore, a deep crafting system, and engaging combat. BDO is known for its stunning graphics and detailed character creation system, as well as its action-based combat system that requires players to actively dodge and move during battles.

The Lord of the Rings Online

    The Lord of the Rings Online is a MMORPG developed and published by Turbine, Inc. It was released in 2007 and is set in J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth. LOTRO features a highly engaging story, a vast and detailed world to explore, a crafting system, and challenging PvE content. It also features a unique music system that allows players to play and perform music in-game.

Eve Online

    Eve Online is a MMORPG developed and published by CCP Games. It was released in 2003 and is set in a sci-fi universe. EVE features a complex and player-driven economy, as well as a deep crafting system and challenging PvP content. It is known for its steep learning curve and the fact that player actions can have significant effects on the game's economy and politics.
